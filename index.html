Why Nairobi?

The Green City under the Sun

Nairobi, the vibrant capital city of Kenya, is a truly remarkable place that captivates visitors with its unique blend of culture, natural beauty, and bustling urban life. Known as the "Green City under the Sun," Nairobi is a great city to explore since it is surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, including Nairobi National Park. Besides, Nairobi's diverse population contributes to its vibrant atmosphere, with a rich tapestry of cultures, traditions, and cuisines to discover. Moreover, the city is a hub of innovation and entrepreneurship, with a thriving tech scene and numerous start-ups making their mark. From world-class museums and art galleries to lively markets and a buzzing nightlife, Nairobi offers a multitude of experiences for every visitor.

My Favourite Places to Visit in Nairobi


Giraffe Genter

The Nairobi Giraffe Center is a must-visit attraction for nature enthusiasts in Nairobi. Visitors can get up close and personal with the majestic Rothschild giraffes, feed them, and learn about conservation efforts to protect these endangered species.

Nairobi National Park

Nairobi National Park is a unique gem that sets Nairobi apart from other major cities. The only national park in the world that borders a capital city and offers visitors the incredible opportunity to observe wildlife.

National Museum of Kenya

The National Museum of Kenya is a cultural gem nestled in Nairobi, showcasing the country's rich heritage and natural history. Visitors can immerse themselves in fascinating exhibitions that highlight diverse aspects of Kenya's past.
